One bag. Every journey, every mile. The Travel Bag 40L is built to take you further. From three-day getaways to week-long excursions, it adapts to the way you move. With a patented strap system that transforms from duffel to backpack on the fly, removable waist straps for comfort, and wide-open packing access, this bag carries it all — and carries it well.
