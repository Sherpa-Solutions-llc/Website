Peak Design Travel Backpack 30L

The rugged expandable daypack for shorter trips & everyday carry.

Peak Design

Travel Backpack 30L

$259.95

The smaller sibling of Peak Design's iconic 45L, the 30L is a rugged, expandable daypack ideal for shorter travel and everyday carry — because No Two Trips The Same™. A large rear hatch provides total access for easy packing. An enlarged top pocket is subdivided with stretch and zip pockets for keys, glasses, and passport. Sits within a sleek, weatherproof 100% recycled 400D nylon canvas shell. Fair Trade Certified and 100% carbon neutral.

Key Features
  • Large rear hatch for full, clam-shell packing access
  • Weatherproof #10 UltraZip built to withstand decades of use
  • Expands from 27L (compressed) to 33L (expanded)
  • Enlarged top pocket with stretch and zip sub-pockets
  • Padded laptop (15") and tablet sleeves
  • Tuck-away shoulder straps with optional hip belt accessory
  • 360° grab handles for versatile carry
  • Cleverly concealed external carry straps
  • Expansive side pockets for water bottles and tripods
  • Meets international carry-on size requirements
  • Fair Trade Certified • 100% carbon neutral • Lifetime warranty
